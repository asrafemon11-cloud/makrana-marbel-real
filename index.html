Our Story

The World's Finest
Natural Stone

Makrana marble is not merely stone — it is history carved from the earth. Quarried from the mines of Makrana, Rajasthan, this pure white calcite marble has adorned the Taj Mahal, the Victoria Memorial, and countless temples and palaces across India for over five centuries.

Our shop brings this legendary stone directly from the quarries to your home, temple, or commercial project. We offer authentic Makrana marble with full traceability — from mine to marble slab.
